Yield Strategy

Deposits are Zapped into Moonwell MetaMorpho (USDC) vaults on Base. Profits are generated via over-collateralized lending and compound every block.

The 76-Hour Loyalty Lock

To prevent flash-loan volatility, deposits are subject to a 76-hour timer. Standard exits receive full principal and profit. Early exits incur a 1% penalty on the total balance.

Zero-Loss Referral Model Commissions are paid out of the protocol's performance fee, never from the recruit's principal deposit. This ensures users always retain 100% of their initial capital.
