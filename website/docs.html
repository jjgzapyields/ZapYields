Is the 80% Tier Unfair?

While high tiers can earn up to 80% commission, it is mathematically impossible for an upline to "take" a recruit's money. Here is why:

The Principal Rule: Referral commissions are only calculated from Net Profit generated during the stay. Your initial deposit is 100% protected by the smart contract.
  • Capital Safety: If you deposit $1,000, you will always be able to withdraw your $1,000 (subject to the 76h lock).
  • Profit Sharing: We only divide the extra money the vault earned for you.
  • Protocol Incentive: The 80% rate rewards high-capital "Whales" for providing liquidity to the ecosystem.
