ZapApy is a decentralized, institutional-grade yield aggregator built on the Base Network. It enables users to deposit standard USDC and instantly access the $100M+ Moonwell MetaMorpho lending market.

ZapApy is designed around a Zero-Loss Principal philosophy: User deposits are 100% protected. Platform fees and referral payouts are deducted exclusively from generated profits, never from the user's initial capital.

The ZapApy Smart Contract handles all financial operations automatically without human intervention.

1. Zap In (Deposit)

  • Minimum Deposit: 10.00 USDC.
  • Action: User funds are instantly routed into the Moonwell MetaMorpho Vault, beginning to earn ~9.1% APY immediately.
  • Loyalty Lock: A 76-hour lock timer begins at the exact moment of deposit.
  • Referral Link: If a user zaps in using a referral link (?ref=0x...), that referrer is permanently linked to the user on the blockchain.

2. Zap Out (Withdrawal)

The system calculates payouts based on the 76-Hour Loyalty Lock:

  • Early Exit (Before 76 Hours): A 1% penalty is applied to the total value to prevent flash-farming bots. 99% of the funds are returned to the user.
  • Standard Exit (After 76 Hours): 100% of the Initial Principal is returned to the user. The generated profit is split three ways:
    • 85% to the User.
    • 10% to the Referrer (paid instantly).
    • 5% to the Protocol Treasury (for sustainability).
