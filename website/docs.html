1. Yield Generation

All deposits are routed into Moonwell MetaMorpho vaults on Base. Yield is generated through over-collateralized lending and incentivized rewards. APY is dynamic and compounds every block.

2. The 76-Hour Loyalty Lock

To ensure liquidity stability, a 76-hour timer starts upon deposit.

  • Standard Withdrawal: Full Principal + Profit.
  • Early Exit: 1% Penalty on total account value.

3. Fee Structure

Protocol sustainability is maintained through a 5% Performance Fee deducted only from generated profits. The principal is never taxed.

Note: Referral commissions are paid by the protocol's share of the yield, not from the recruit's principal. This ensures a "Zero-Loss" experience for all participants.
